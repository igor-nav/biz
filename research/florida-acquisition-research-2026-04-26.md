## BBB Due Diligence Checklist

Listing names are often confidential until NDA, so run this after receiving the actual business name:

1. Search bbb.org with exact business name and city.
2. Check rating, complaint history, years in business, and accreditation.
3. Read recent complaints and resolutions.
4. Cross-check with Google Reviews, Yelp, and industry-specific review sites.
